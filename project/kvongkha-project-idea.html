Karissa's Project Idea

My project idea stemmed from my love for food. I knew I wanted to do something around creating a resource of recipes and various dishes. At home, I was brought up eating SEA food. My mother is an amazing cook who is my primary resource for creating content. There are also Youtubers and recipes that people share online. Of course I will have to ask their permission to use their images and reference inforamtion.

About
